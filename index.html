Why ShadeChat

Privacy isn't a setting. It's the default.

Every feature below exists to put you in control of what you share, and with whom.

End-to-End Encryption

Every message, call, and file is encrypted on your device before it ever leaves — built on the same protocol trusted by leading secure messengers, not a homemade alternative.

One Privacy Dashboard

Last seen, read receipts, online status, profile photo — every privacy control lives in one clear screen, not scattered across nested settings menus.

Contact-Level Privacy

Set different privacy rules for every person you talk to. Share more with close friends, less with everyone else — your call, per contact.

Secret Chats & Panic Mode

Lock sensitive conversations behind biometrics, keep them out of your main chat list entirely, or trigger Panic Mode to hide everything sensitive in an instant.

Self-Destruct & View-Once

Send messages that disappear on your schedule, or photos that can only be opened once. Once it's gone from our servers, it's gone.

No Ads. No Tracking. Ever.

We don't build a profile on you, we don't sell your data, and we don't show you ads. ShadeChat makes money from the app, not from you.

Security

Built around one rule:
your data is yours.

ShadeChat is designed so your conversations stay between you and the people you're talking to — not stored, sold, or scanned. Our servers only ever relay encrypted data they cannot read.

  1. 01

    Encrypted end-to-end

    Messages are encrypted on your device and decrypted only on the recipient's — our servers never hold a readable copy.

  2. 02

    No ad tracking

    ShadeChat doesn't build a behavioral profile on you to sell to advertisers. There are no ads to target you with in the first place.

  3. 03

    You control your data

    Delete a conversation and it's gone. Delete your account and your data is removed on a clear, published schedule — no hidden backups you didn't ask for.
